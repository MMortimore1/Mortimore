About Us

Mortimore Rotorsports is a startup company in aeronautical car racing founded by Marc Mortimore. As a third-year engineering student, Marc has a passion for creating cutting-edge technology for high-speed air racing, inspired by games like Wipeout.

At Mortimore Rotorsports, we aspire to enter the EXA airspeeder series and bring the thrill and excitement of high-speed air racing to reality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design and develop flying race cars that are not only fast and agile, but utilise novel technology and ideas. We strive to push the limits of technology and engineering to create race cars that can compete at the highest level of air racing.
